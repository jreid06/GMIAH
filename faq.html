FAQ

agents can't?

You know how your family doctor sometimes has to refer you to a specialist? Well, think of us as the "specialist" when it comes to unusual estate situations. Estate agents can handle many straightforward home sales, but they have their rules and limits. If a family comes along that does not fit into those rules, then they say "Sorry, we can't help you."

Because we're the specialists in dealing with difficult credit problems, small down payments, and other issues, we pride ourselves in finding solutions when others just throw up their hands.

Q. How does your "Rent to Own" Program work?

This is the "best-kept secret" in America! We have a regular supply of nice homes where you can move in immediately. Then, while you enjoy the benefits of living there, you pay rent, a portion of which goes toward your eventual mortgage on the home.

Yes, you read that correctly: You get to move into a nice home; you pay rent while you fix your credit, or decide whether you want to buy the home; and a portion of your rent goes toward your mortgage.

Some of our plans even allow you to "lock in" today's purchase price for the home! That means you don't have to sweat whether home prices are growing faster than your ability to get a good mortgage.
